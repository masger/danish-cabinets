# Consolidated Results: The European Cabinet Survival Study (v3)

## Executive Summary
This iteration strips all biological mortality data to focus exclusively on political career survival. We find that political termination (firing) occurs at a rate of 4.2 to 21.9 per 100 person-years across Europe. While the "Transport Curse" remains a significant finding in Denmark and the UK, a pooled analysis across 10 nations reveals that **Transport** and **Finance** portfolios carry the highest hazard ratios relative to Health (Ref). All tables now reflect a harmonized "Analyzable Cohort" of ministers with valid tenure data.
